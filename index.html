About Us

We founded TAKUMI Inc. (store name JOLIJOU) with a simple mission: to share the best of Japan with the world. Under the guidance of our CEO, Shohei Okazaki, we deliver Japan’s unique strengths in high-quality reuse and recycling – along with the spirit of “mottainai” – to shoppers across the globe. In Japan, mottainai is a cherished concept that means “don’t let things go to waste.” It’s about deeply respecting the value of items, reducing waste, and giving quality goods a second life.

Embracing this spirit, we also work hand-in-hand with our group’s disability support program. Through processes like expert bag repair and cleaning, we create meaningful employment opportunities, striving to build a sustainable society where everyone can contribute.

By spreading this cycle of renewal and respect for resources, we hope to introduce people worldwide to a piece of Japanese culture – and in doing so, foster understanding and goodwill across borders. That is our vision.

Authenticity Guarantee

You can shop with confidence at JOLIJOU. We source all our products from trusted AACD member suppliers (AACD is the Association Against Counterfeit Product Distribution in Japan, dedicated to eliminating fake goods). On top of that, our in-house team of experts – two specialists with over 10 years of industry experience each – rigorously inspects every item for authenticity. Only pieces that pass this strict dual-check are offered for sale.

For even greater peace of mind, we can provide a third-party Certificate of Authenticity upon request. This service is available to any customer who wants independent verification of their purchase. Just let our staff know, and we’ll be happy to arrange it for you.
